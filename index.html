Frequently Asked Questions
What is RSGP? ▼
RSGP is Cambria's ecosystem token designed to deepen the connection between players, guilds, and contributors. Staked RSGP unlocks features like reduced fees and governance participation.
Which tokens can I deposit? ▼
You can deposit USDC, ETH, or USDT on Ethereum mainnet. All deposits are converted to a USD-equivalent value for allocation calculation.
How is my allocation determined? ▼
Your RSGP allocation depends on your deposit size relative to total deposits, your onchain history, Ethos score, and other engagement factors within the Cambria ecosystem.
When can I claim my tokens? ▼
Tokens will be claimable after the Token Generation Event (TGE). 25% is available at TGE, with the remaining 75% vesting linearly over 6 months.
Do I need RSGP to play Cambria? ▼
No, RSGP is not required to play Cambria. The games are accessible without the token, but holding RSGP provides additional benefits and governance rights.
